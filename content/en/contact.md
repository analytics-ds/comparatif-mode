## Write to us

For any question, topic suggestion, error report or editorial collaboration proposal, you can reach us by email.

**General email**: contact@comparatif-mode.fr

## Response time

We respond to all requests within 3 to 5 business days. Please specify the subject of your message (editorial question, product test proposal, error report) to help us process it faster.

## Editorial proposals

Guest article proposals, collaborations or partnerships are reviewed case by case. We prioritize independent and factual content, in line with our editorial policy detailed on the [About](/en/about/) page.

## Correction and right of reply

If you spot a factual error in an article, outdated information, or wish to exercise a right of reply, please contact us with the specific details. Corrections are published as soon as possible.
