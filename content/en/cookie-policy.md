## What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your terminal (computer, phone, tablet) when visiting a website. It lets the site remember certain information (language preferences, session ID).

## Cookies used on Comparatif mode

Comparatif mode uses a limited number of cookies, strictly necessary for site operation and audience measurement.

### Technical cookies

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the site (language choice, session). They cannot be disabled.

### Analytics cookies

We use an anonymous analytics tool to understand how visitors use the site. Data collected is aggregated and cannot identify a specific person.

## Consent

On your first visit, a banner informs you of the use of cookies. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing cookies from your browser.

## Manage your cookies

You can manage cookies from your browser:

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

## Retention period

Technical cookies are kept for the duration of the session. Analytics cookies are kept for a maximum of 13 months.
