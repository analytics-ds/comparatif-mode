## Data collected

Comparatif mode collects a minimum of personal data. Data that may be collected includes:

- IP address (anonymized) and browsing data via analytics tools
- Email address if you write to us via the Contact page

## Purposes

Data is used only to:

- Measure site traffic anonymously
- Reply to received messages
- Ensure technical security of the site

No data is sold or transmitted to third parties for commercial purposes.

## Retention period

Anonymous navigation logs are kept for 12 months. Received emails are kept for 3 years for editorial follow-up.

## Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to object to processing
- Right to portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@comparatif-mode.fr
