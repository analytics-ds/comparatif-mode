## Our mission

Comparatif mode is an independent blog dedicated to men and women fashion comparisons. Our goal is simple: help everyone make informed choices about clothing, accessories and brands, without jargon or marketing promises.

## Our approach

Every comparison and buying guide published on Comparatif mode is based on:

- Factual product analysis (cut, fabric, origin, price)
- Context-aware advice based on body types and uses
- Transparent comparisons of available market options
- Full editorial independence from the brands mentioned

We do not receive any compensation from the brands cited. Articles are not sponsored.

## Our topics

We cover five main topics: product comparisons, buying guides, fashion news, women's fashion and men's fashion. From basics to statement pieces, we cover the entire clothing choice process.
