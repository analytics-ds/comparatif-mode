## Burberry Heritage Kensington: the absolute reference

Burberry invented waterproof gabardine in 1879 and the military trench in 1914. The Heritage Kensington model revives the brand's historical codes: 100% cotton gabardine with patented water-repellent treatment, 10-button double-breasted closure, epaulets, D-ring on the belt, visible Burberry check lining. The cut is wide, belted, with length reaching the calves.

A Burberry Heritage's durability is exceptional: with classic care (annual dry cleaning, water-repellent retouching every 5 years), the trench passes 30 years of regular use without structural loss. It is a heritage purchase that gets passed down.

- **Price**: 2,190 euros (long Kensington), 1,790 euros (mid-length Chelsea)
- **Fabric**: 100% cotton gabardine, viscose Burberry check lining
- **Sizes**: 32 to 48
- **Cuts**: Kensington (long), Chelsea (mid), Pimlico (short)

## Mackintosh Roslin: the modern British alternative

Mackintosh, founded in Scotland in 1823, invented waterproof waxed fabric. The Roslin model offers a hand-tied waxed cotton trench, more contemporary than Burberry with a straight mid-length cut, raglan sleeves, and a minimalist tailored collar. The waxed material offers superior waterproofing to treated gabardine.

- **Price**: 1,290 euros
- **Fabric**: 100% waxed cotton, cotton lining
- **Sizes**: 34 to 44

Mackintosh Roslin suits silhouettes that prefer a modern straight cut to the belted classic. Less formal than Burberry, it works easily with jeans and sneakers or over a dress.

## A.P.C. Marc: Parisian minimalism

A.P.C. offers the most coherent beige trench in the mid-premium segment. The Marc model is cut in 100% cotton gabardine, straight cut without tied belt (loose belt at the waist), simple 6-button closure, discreet shirt collar. The aesthetic focuses on sobriety and durability.

- **Price**: 595 euros
- **Fabric**: 100% cotton gabardine
- **Sizes**: XS to XL

A.P.C. Marc is the ideal choice for a durable minimalist wardrobe, without ostentatious signatures. It wears for 10 years without dating.

## Sessun Marguerite: the current oversized style

Sessun, a Marseille brand, offers a modern beige trench with a lightly oversized cut that moves away from the historical belted standard. The Marguerite uses a blended cotton-polyester gabardine (70-30), more accessible, with mid-length and raglan sleeves.

- **Price**: 395 euros
- **Fabric**: cotton-polyester gabardine
- **Sizes**: 34 to 44

Sessun Marguerite suits silhouettes looking for a chic casual trench, worn open over jeans or belted over a midi dress.

## Claudie Pierlot, Mango and Uniqlo: the accessible options

**Claudie Pierlot Gabardine** (495 euros): classic belted cut, defined waist, cotton-polyester. Feminine and structured.

**Mango Premium Trench** (179 euros): cotton blend, long belted cut, correct finishes for the price.

**Uniqlo Trench Coat** (99.90 euros): water-repellent polyester-cotton, mid-length straight cut, plain lining. Best value-for-money under 100 euros. Stick with beige or camel colors, avoid colored versions that reveal the synthetic finish.

## How to choose a women's beige trench

Three objective criteria guide the choice.

### The fabric

- **100% cotton gabardine** (Burberry, A.P.C.): reference, lasts 20+ years
- **Waxed cotton** (Mackintosh): waterproof, more matte appearance
- **70-30 cotton-polyester** (Sessun, Claudie Pierlot): good price-durability compromise
- **Polyester-cotton** (Uniqlo, Mango): acceptable under 200 euros, 3-5 years
- **100% polyester**: avoid above 80 euros

### The cut

- **Classic belted** (Burberry, Claudie Pierlot): flatters all body types with tied belt
- **Minimalist straight** (A.P.C., Mackintosh): lean silhouettes or H-shape
- **Oversized** (Sessun, Zara): slim silhouettes wanting volume
- **Short** (Burberry Pimlico): petite sizes, under 1.65m

### The budget

- Entry (100 to 200 euros): Uniqlo, Mango
- Mid (300 to 500 euros): Sessun, Claudie Pierlot, Mango Premium
- High (500 to 800 euros): A.P.C., COS Heritage
- Premium (1,000 to 2,500 euros): Mackintosh, Burberry
