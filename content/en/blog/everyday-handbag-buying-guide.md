## Quick take

1. The **size** must hold laptop, notebook and essentials without deforming
2. **Full-grain leather** is the most durable investment
3. **Inside organization** saves time every morning
4. An **adjustable strap** allows hand, shoulder or crossbody carry

## The size

For an active life, pick a bag 35 to 42 cm wide, able to fit a 13-inch laptop, an A5 notebook and essentials. Avoid decorative mini-bags that add nothing in daily use.

## The material

Full-grain leather ages well and gains character. Coated canvas (waxed cotton, impregnated canvas) is lighter but less durable. Synthetics should be avoided beyond 6 months of intensive use.

## Inside organization

A good work bag has: a padded laptop pocket, 2-3 zip pockets for small items, a pen holder and a reinforced bottom. No organization turns the bag into a black hole.

## The strap

Go for an adjustable, detachable strap to switch between hand, shoulder and crossbody carry depending on the situation.
