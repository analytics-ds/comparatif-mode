## Quick take

1. The **white tee** and **white shirt** form the two tops foundations
2. **Straight jeans** and **black tailored pants** cover 80% of bottom needs
3. The **black blazer** and **beige trench** are the two key jackets
4. A **cashmere sweater** adds the essential warm layer

## The plain white tee

Organic cotton, straight cut, round neck. Choose a weight above 180 g/m2 to avoid transparency. Reference brands: Uniqlo Supima, Petit Bateau, Asket.

## Straight jeans

Straight high-waisted cut, raw or medium wash. Avoid trend cuts that date quickly. Levi's 501 Original, A.P.C. New Standard, Agolde 90s Pinch Waist are safe choices.

## The white shirt

Tightly woven cotton poplin, straight or slightly fitted cut. Avoid shiny finishes. The White Shirt by Charvet, Uniqlo Supima, Equipment Slim Signature.

## The black blazer

Virgin wool, straight or slightly fitted cut, structured shoulders. This is the first piece to invest in. Theory, Joseph, Filippa K, The Row for higher budgets.

## The beige trench

Cotton gabardine, double-breasted, tied belt. Burberry is the historical reference, but Mackintosh, A.P.C. and Sessun offer quality alternatives.
