## The 4 rules for wearing an oversized blazer

### Rule 1: balance the volume

Fundamental principle: if the top is oversized, the bottom must be fitted. A wide blazer over loose pants creates a structureless silhouette. Combinations that work:

- Oversized blazer + high-waisted straight jeans + white t-shirt
- Oversized blazer + cigarette tailored pants + fine shirt
- Oversized blazer + midi pencil skirt + fitted top
- Oversized blazer + tailored shorts + opaque tights
- Oversized blazer alone over a slip dress or fitted midi dress

### Rule 2: define the waist

An oversized blazer can flatten the silhouette if the waist is not visible. Three techniques to restore definition:

- **Tuck the top into the bottom**: t-shirt into high jeans, create a visible line at the waist
- **Belt the blazer**: tie a thin belt above or below the blazer
- **Open the blazer**: let a vertical line appear to elongate the silhouette

### Rule 3: adapt the cut to the body type

- **A-shape body** (wider hips): defined-shoulder blazer to balance, avoid blazers cinched at the waist
- **V-shape body** (wider shoulders): natural-shoulder blazer without padding, straight cut
- **H-shape body** (straight): belted or not, all cuts work
- **8-shape body** (defined waist): fitted blazer to highlight the waist, or oversized with a belt
- **O-shape body** (round): mid-length open blazer over a dark vertical base

### Rule 4: play with sleeve length

Oversized blazer sleeves often extend past hands or wrists. Three options:

- Let them fall over the hands for a deliberate oversized effect
- Roll them to forearms to lighten the silhouette and show wrists
- Have sleeves adjusted by a tailor (20 to 35 euros)

## How to combine an oversized blazer by occasion

**At the office (business casual dress code)**: black or navy oversized blazer + cigarette tailored pants + white shirt + derbies or loafers. Remove the belt for a more formal look.

**At an evening event**: beige or camel oversized blazer + satin slip dress + heeled sandals + leather clutch. An elegant look without effort.

**Daily casual**: Mocha Mousse oversized blazer + straight jeans + white t-shirt + white sneakers + leather bucket bag.

**For a casual chic event**: sage green oversized blazer + pleated midi skirt + flat mary-jane shoes + thin tied belt.

**While traveling**: oversized blazer without structure (no shoulder pads) in wool blend + stretch jeans + cotton t-shirt + sneakers. Easily unwrinkles and adapts to all situations.

## Caring for an oversized blazer

A wool or wool-blend blazer requires minimal but regular care:

- **Air out** after each wear, hang on a wide hanger
- **Brush** with a clothes brush to remove dust and particles
- **Straighten the sleeves** to avoid permanent elbow creases
- **Dry clean** once or twice a year, no more
- **Avoid direct iron**: use a steam de-wrinkler from a distance

A well-cared-for pure wool blazer lasts 10 to 15 years. In wool blend, count 5 to 7 years with regular wear.
