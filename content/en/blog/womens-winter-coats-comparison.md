## Quick take

1. The **Max Mara Manuela** is the camel cocoon coat reference in wool and cashmere
2. The **Sandro Penelope** offers a minimalist straight cut at a more accessible price
3. The **Maje Gremio** features a feminine belted structured cut
4. The **Claudie Pierlot Gilberto** appeals with its oversized straight trend-driven cut

## Max Mara Manuela

The great camel cocoon coat classic. Virgin wool and cashmere, wide cut falling mid-calf, tailored collar. A timeless fashion investment. Indicative price: 2,290 euros.

## Sandro Penelope

Wool blend, minimalist straight cut, mid-length. More accessible than Max Mara, it keeps Parisian elegance. Indicative price: 645 euros.

## Maje Gremio

Wool and polyamide, structured belted cut, knee-length. The belt defines the waist and flatters A-shape or 8-shape silhouettes. Indicative price: 595 euros.

## Claudie Pierlot Gilberto

Wool blend, oversized straight cut, dropped shoulders. Perfect for a chic casual look. Indicative price: 495 euros.
