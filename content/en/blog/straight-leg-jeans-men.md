## Quick take

1. The **Levi's 501** is the universal benchmark for straight-leg jeans, across all body types
2. The **A.P.C. Petit New Standard** stands out for its Japanese raw denim quality
3. The **Nudie Jeans Gritty Jackson** delivers a relaxed straight cut in organic cotton
4. The **Uniqlo Regular Fit Selvedge Jeans** offer the best value for money in this comparison

## Levi's 501 Original

Classic straight cut, 14 oz denim, button fly. The 501 remains the absolute reference for straight-leg jeans. It fits all body types thanks to its moderately wide thigh cut. Indicative price: 110 euros.

## A.P.C. Petit New Standard

Japanese raw denim 13 oz, slightly slimmer cut than the 501. Ideal for minimalist style and a personalized fade after several months of wear. Indicative price: 195 euros.

## Nudie Jeans Gritty Jackson

100% organic cotton, relaxed straight cut, mid-high waist. The Swedish brand offers free lifetime repairs, a plus for a long-lasting pair. Indicative price: 169 euros.

## Uniqlo Regular Fit Selvedge Jeans

Kaihara selvedge denim (Japan), standard straight cut, clean finish. The best value for money in this comparison. Indicative price: 49.90 euros.
