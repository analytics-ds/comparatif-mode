## Quick take

1. **Workwear** returns with modernized cuts
2. **Earth tones** dominate: terracotta, sand, olive
3. **Loose cuts** progressively replace slim
4. The **teddy jacket** makes a big comeback

## Revisited workwear

Spring 2026 runways (Lemaire, Our Legacy, Marni) bring back moleskin jackets, overalls and carpenter pants. Inspiration comes from American and French mid-20th-century workwear, updated with more fluid cuts.

## Earth tones

Terracotta, sand, ochre and olive form the dominant palette. These warm tones replace the cooler shades of previous seasons. They work in total look or as a strong accent on a key piece.

## Loose cuts

Relaxed fit pants, oversized shirts and wide jackets mark a break from the 2010-2020 slim fit. The volume is controlled, never exaggerated.

## Teddy meets workwear

The varsity teddy jacket returns, crossed with workwear aesthetics. Schott, A.P.C., Our Legacy offer updated models in wool blend or heavy cotton.
