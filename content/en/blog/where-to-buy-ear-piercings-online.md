## How to choose the site to buy ear piercings online

Four objective criteria guide platform choice.

### The jewelry material

This is the main criterion for ear health and jewelry durability. Material hierarchy from least to most durable:

- **316L surgical steel**: acceptable for healed piercings, may contain traces of nickel. Avoid if sensitive skin.
- **925 silver** (92.5% silver): durable, may darken over time, requires regular cleaning. Good price-quality compromise.
- **1-micron gold plated** (entry-level mass market): lasts 6 to 12 months in daily wear before the layer wears out.
- **3-micron gold plated** (Nebuleuse Bijoux, Lou Yetu): lasts 2 to 3 years in daily wear. The recommended standard.
- **Vermeil** (925 silver plated with 3-micron minimum gold): equivalent to thick gold plated, more traceable as the base is solid silver.
- **14 carat gold** (58% gold, Mejuri, Astrid & Miyu): unlimited durability, does not tarnish, mid-range price.
- **18 carat gold** (75% gold, Maria Tash, Histoire d'Or): luxury reference, unlimited durability, resale value.
- **Grade 23 titanium**: only material recommended for initial placement and healing, hypoallergenic.

### The piercing area

Not all areas of the ear accept the same jewelry. Verify that the site indicates the recommended area for each model:

- **Lobe**: most jewelry (studs, hoops, drops) work
- **Helix** (upper cartilage): straight labrets, fine hoops 6 to 10 mm diameter
- **Conch** (central cartilage): long labrets, hoops 10 to 14 mm
- **Daith** (internal central cartilage): curved hoops or hearts
- **Tragus** (front of ear canal): short labrets, small hoops
- **Rook** (upper cartilage): curved bars, hearts, small hoops

### The price and durability

Calibrate the budget by use:

- First purchase or test: 15 to 30 euros (Nebuleuse Bijoux entry level, Lou Yetu)
- Standard daily wear: 30 to 80 euros (Nebuleuse Bijoux, Lou Yetu, Mejuri)
- Durable investment: 80 to 250 euros (Mejuri 14 carat gold, Astrid & Miyu, Aristocrazy)
- Signature piece or wedding: 250 euros and more (Maria Tash, Histoire d'Or 18 carat gold)

### Return and warranty policy

A tried piercing rarely remains returnable for hygiene reasons. Verify each site's exact policy:

- Nebuleuse Bijoux, Mejuri, Astrid & Miyu: 30-day return on unworn models (sealed packaging)
- Maria Tash: 14-day return on standard models, no return on custom
- Histoire d'Or, Aristocrazy: in-store return within 30 days
