## Quick take

1. The **Common Projects Achilles Low** is the minimalist premium reference
2. The **Veja Campo** combines style and environmental responsibility
3. The **Adidas Stan Smith** remains the accessible timeless icon
4. The **Nike Court Legacy** offers the best value for money

## Common Projects Achilles Low

Italian full-grain leather, minimalist cut, gold signature at the back. The reference for premium white sneakers since 2004. Indicative price: 440 euros.

## Veja Campo

Chrome-free leather, Amazonian rubber sole, production in Brazil. Style close to Common Projects at a more accessible price with a clear ecological commitment. Indicative price: 130 euros.

## Adidas Stan Smith

Synthetic leather or vegan leather depending on version, iconic green heel. The 1970s icon, updated. Indicative price: 100 euros.

## Nike Court Legacy

Canvas or leather depending on models, rubber sole, discreet swoosh logo. Affordable alternative to premium models. Indicative price: 85 euros.
