## Quick take

1. The **shirt dress** flatters H and 8 body types
2. The **flared midi** is ideal for A body types
3. The **wrap cut** defines the waist for O body types
4. The **A-line dress** suits V body types without structuring shoulders

## Shirt dress

Straight or slightly fitted cut, shirt collar, front buttons, tie belt. Suits H, 8 and A body types. A.P.C., Sessun, Sezane offer very versatile models.

## Flared midi

Fitted high waist, flared skirt that widens below the hips. Ideal for A body types. The cut masks hip width and highlights bust slenderness.

## Wrap dress

Side-tied wrap, adjustable to body type. Flattering for O, 8 and H body types. Diane von Furstenberg remains the historical reference.

## A-line dress

Flared line from shoulders, without defining the waist. Ideal for V body types looking to balance shoulder width. Simple cut, often featuring graphic details.
