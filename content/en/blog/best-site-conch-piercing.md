## How to choose your conch piercing jewel

Four objective criteria guide the choice.

### Phase: healing or daily wear

- **Healing (0 to 12 months after placement)**: Grade 23 titanium exclusively, simple straight labrets. Choose Nebuleuse Bijoux, Neometal, or Astrid & Miyu
- **Healed (after 6 to 12 months)**: all materials accepted, free choice. Nebuleuse, Mejuri, Maria Tash by budget

### Material

- **Grade 23 ASTM F-136 titanium**: essential for healing, 45 to 220 euros
- **Solid 14-18 carat gold**: unlimited durability, does not tarnish, 80 to 800 euros
- **3-micron gold plated**: lasts 2 to 3 years of daily wear, 30 to 80 euros
- **925 silver**: may darken, good price-quality compromise, 20 to 60 euros
- **316L surgical steel**: acceptable on healed conch, 15 to 40 euros

### Diameter and gauge

- **10 mm diameter**: small ears, discreet effect
- **12 mm diameter**: universal standard
- **14 mm diameter**: wide ears, statement effect
- **1.2 mm gauge**: standard for healed conch
- **1.6 mm gauge**: conch pierced in wider gauge (rare in France)

### Style and desired effect

- **Minimalist**: fine gold or silver hoop, Mejuri or Nebuleuse
- **Statement**: wide hoop set or with charms, Maria Tash or Nebuleuse hearts
- **Graphic**: original labrets, Le Coeur en Braille or Astrid & Miyu
